FAQs

What is BMI ?
The BMI is a measure of your weight relative to your height and waist circumference measures abdominal fat. Combining these with information about your additional risk factors yields your risk for developing obesity-associated diseases. Weight loss will lower BMI.
How is BMI calculated ?
BMI calculations are based on your gender , height and weight. Check out our BMI calculator tool to calculate your own BMI.
Which are the costs of your weight loss programs ?
The cost of our programs varies depending on the ocasion. For more information call our client support number.
What forms of payment do you accept ?

What type of methods are being used to help your clients ?
Our fasting programs are based on the concept of a Protein Sparing Modified Fast, in which we meet the body’s protein, vitamin, mineral, and fluid needs. When utilized appropriately with medical supervision, these programs are both effective for weight loss and quite safe. We monitor blood pressure, blood work, rate of weight loss, and other variables, and adjust as needed to ensure safe success.
What can I do to maintain my weight loss ?
We also offer a maintenance program. Most diets “fail” because the diet comes to an end. People do a short-term fat diet, then go back to the old habits. Only by addressing the habits that are getting you into trouble can we prevent weight regain. Your dietitian will work with you to develop realistic, achievable goals, and to implement a plan with you to reach and stay at those goals.
